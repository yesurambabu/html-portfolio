The Best Movies According to Mattupalli. Yesu Rambabu

My top 3 movies of all-time.

This is a great film by S. S. Rajamouli. Both parts were amazing and the acting by Prabhas was like never before ever after. He acted in such a way that no one in the entire world can replace that character apart from Prabhas.

Pushpa The Rise:

This is my favourite movie. I love the way Sukumar and Allu Arjun worked to make that masterpeice which proved by bringing the National Award for Allu Arjuns best acting.

Animal Part 1:

This is also my facourite movie. The way of taking the movie by the Sandeep Reddy Vanga was astonishing. Also the unexpected mesmerising performance of Ranbir Kapoor was also astonishing.
